DROP 01 is for the people, and the company should never be bigger than the artist.

We are here to help independent designers grow into the strongest version of themselves. Creativity is emotion, self-expression, and trust shared in public. We support production, release planning, and distribution so the work can move from idea to garment without losing the person behind it. Small, demand-led drops keep the process intentional, reduce waste, and let community decide what deserves to become real.
